## 1. Overview

Percentage Calculator ("the App") is a free utility app published by GrizPlay ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
The App does not ask you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

### 2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device
The App stores the following data **exclusively on your device** using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Calculation history (last 20 entries) | So you can review and restore recent calculations |
| App preferences (theme, currency, decimal places, haptic, real-time calc) | To remember your display and calculation settings |
| Onboarding completion flag | To show the welcome screens only on first launch |

You can delete all locally stored calculation history at any time via **Settings → Clear History** inside the App.

### 2.3 Information Collected by Third Parties (Advertising)
The App displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob** (a service of Google LLC). AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device to serve relevant ads, including:

- **Advertising ID (GAID):** A resettable, non-permanent device identifier used for interest-based advertising.
- **Device information:** Device model, OS version, language, time zone, and screen resolution.
- **Interaction data:** Whether an ad was shown, clicked, or dismissed.
- **IP address:** Used to determine approximate geographic region for ad targeting and fraud prevention.
- **Coarse location:** Derived from IP address only; the App does **not** request GPS or precise location permission.

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. We do not have access to this raw data.

We do not collect personal data directly. The locally stored preferences and history are used solely to provide app functionality — nothing is sent to our servers.

## 5. Permissions Requested

The App requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| `INTERNET` | Required for AdMob to load and display advertisements |
| `VIBRATE` | Provides haptic feedback when tapping buttons (can be disabled in Settings) |
| `com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID` | Required by Google AdMob to access the Advertising ID on Android 13+ |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` *(Android 13+ only)* | Required to show optional re-engagement reminder notifications. The App asks for permission before scheduling any notification; you can decline or revoke this at any time in device Settings. |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Used to reschedule any pending notification reminders after a device restart, so they are not silently lost |
| `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` *(maxSdkVersion 32)* | Legacy permission auto-included by the React Native build system for Android 12 and below; not used by the App |
| `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` *(maxSdkVersion 32)* | Legacy permission auto-included by the React Native build system for Android 12 and below; not used by the App |

The App does **not** request access to:
- Camera or microphone
- Contacts or call logs
- Precise or coarse GPS location
- SMS or phone state
- Biometrics or device sensors beyond vibration
